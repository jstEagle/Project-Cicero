### $$e^{i\theta} = \cos\theta + i\sin\theta $$
_______

Let $\cos\theta + i\sin\theta = z$
$$\frac{dz}{d\theta} = -\sin\theta + i\cos\theta$$
$$i^2\sin\theta + i\cos\theta\quad i^2=-1$$
$$=i(i\sin\theta + \cos\theta)$$
$$=iz$$
If $z = e^{i\theta + c}$ 
$$\frac{dz}{d\theta} =ie^{i\theta + c}$$
$$=iz$$
