$$\int 2x(x^2 + 1)^3*dx$$
$$\int 2x*u^3*dx$$
$$x^2 + 1 = u$$
$$\frac{du}{dx} = 2x$$
$$du = 2x * dx$$
$$\therefore \int u^3 * du$$
or 
$$\frac{du}{dx} = 2x$$
$$dx = \frac{du}{2x}$$
$$\therefore \int \cancel{2x} * u^3 * \frac{du}{\cancel{2x}}$$
$$ = \int u^3*du$$
$$ = \frac{u^4}4 + c$$
$$= \frac{(x^2 + 1)^4}4 + c$$
