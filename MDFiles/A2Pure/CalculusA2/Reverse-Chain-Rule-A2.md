### $$\int g(x)^n *dx = \frac{g(x)^{n+1}}{n+1} * \frac{1}{g'(x)} $$
### $$\int(4x+5)^6 * dx = \frac{(4x+5)^7}{7} * \frac{1}{4} $$
____
### Example
### $$\int 2x(x^2 + 1)^3 * dx$$
$$let\ (x^2 + 1) = u$$
$$\frac{du}{dx} = 2x$$
$$\therefore du = 2x*dx$$
$$\int 2x(u)^3 * dx$$
$$ = \int u^3 * du$$
$$= \frac{u^4}{4}$$
$$= \frac{(x^2 + 1)^4}{4}$$
