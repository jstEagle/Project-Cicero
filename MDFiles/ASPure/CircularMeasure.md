### Radians
Measured in $\pi$

- Full circle = $2\pi$ radians
- 180$^o$ = $\pi$ radians
- 90$^o$ = $\frac{\pi}2$ radians

#### $$degrees * \frac{\pi}{180} = radians$$
#### $$360^o * \frac{\pi}{180} = 2\pi$$
#### $$radians * \frac{180}{\pi} = degrees$$
