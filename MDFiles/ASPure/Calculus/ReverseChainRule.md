[[Chain Rule]]
### $$\int (ax+b)^n\ dx = \frac{(ax+b)^{n+1}}{n+1} * \frac1{a} + c$$

_______
#### Example
$$ \int (\frac{x}4 + 3)^3\ dx$$
$$ \frac{(\frac{x}4 + 3)^4}{4} * \frac{1}{\frac14} + c$$
$$ = (\frac{x}4 + 3)^4 + c$$
_______

#### Example
$$\int \frac2{3(x-4)^5}\ dx$$
$$\frac23 \int (x-4)^{-5}\ dx$$
$$= \frac23 * \frac{(x-4)^{-4}}{-4} * \frac11 + c$$
$$ = \frac{2(x-4)^{-4}}{-12} + c$$
$$ = -\frac{1}{6(x-4)^4} + c$$
